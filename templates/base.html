Orcapella Digital/Kogi State Ministry of Commerce and Industry Investment and Entrepreneurs Summit 2021

The Investment and Entrepreneurs Summit 2021 is one of the scheduled activities for the forthcoming event titled; FROM KOGI TO NIGERIA! The summit is going to be a PPP (Public-Private-Partnership).

The Investment and Entrepreneurs Summit aims to introduce new paradigms on finance and the economy of Kogi state which is inspired by Naija priority Initiative, a project of Orcapella digital in collaboration with Ministry of Commerce and Industry Kogi State, to present transformational businesses as new models for poverty alleviation and sustainable development in Kogi state. The summit will create a platform for networking among business owners in Kogi State, generating four-fold capital (human, social, natural and financial) as new models to also empower SME's in the state.

The summit will also bring together chief executives/top level operators from the private sector and very senior Government officials to discuss how best to develop Kogi state economy and monitor the progress that is being made. The main focus of the summit is the short to medium term policy direction while giving priority to the exploits of Governor Yahaya Adoza Bello's Administration in the area of internal revenue generation in the context of the evolving global economy.
